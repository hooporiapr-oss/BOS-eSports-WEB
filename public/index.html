The Training System

4 COGNITIVE LANES

Each drill isolates a distinct cognitive domain that peer-reviewed research identifies as a predictor of competitive esports performance. Progressive difficulty from Bronze to Radiant.

GAME 01
👁
STROBE DRILL
Visual Processing

Track and eliminate targets through strobe disruptions that black out your field of vision. Train your brain to process faster with incomplete information.

GAME 02
🎯
FLICKSHOT
Precision & Speed

Rapid flick aim training with moving targets, shrinking hit zones, and distance-based scoring. Build the motor precision that wins duels.

GAME 03
🧠
SPLIT FOCUS
Cognitive Control

Divided attention under rule switches. Track targets, ignore decoys, adapt when the rules change mid-round. The executive function workout.

GAME 04
⏱
CLUTCH TIMER
Decision Quality

Make the right call under shrinking time windows with stacked rules and conflict resolution. The skill that separates clutch from choke.
